About the Class

The 2nd Year ICS (Intermediate in Computer Science) program with Physics and Mathematics is designed for students aspiring to build a strong foundation in technology, science, and logical thinking. This course prepares you for higher education in fields like software engineering, data science, and physics-related disciplines.

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Focus: Computer Science, Physics, Mathematics
  • Eligibility: Completion of 1st Year ICS or equivalent

Subjects

Our curriculum includes the following core subjects:

  • Computer Science: Programming, Data Structures, Databases
  • Physics: Mechanics, Electromagnetism, Modern Physics
  • Mathematics: Calculus, Algebra, Geometry
  • Compulsory: English, Urdu, and Islamic Studies/Pakistan Studies
